[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
When the world powers down and the porch light fades
I pull you close in the twilight shades
Just the sound of our breath in the silent room
You are the spark in the midnight gloom

[Verse 1]
We’ve been running on empty through the daylight hours
Chasing the clock under concrete towers
But out here right now, with the curtains drawn
We have a quiet place until the dawn

[Pre-Chorus]
The embers are burning, casting gold on your skin
This is the moment where the magic begins
I am leaning in softly, feeling the heat
Matching the rhythm of your slow heartbeat

[Chorus]
We glow in the dark, just a flicker and flame
Whispering secrets without any shame
No neon signs, no moon in the sky
Just the fire I see when I look in your eye

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, we glow in the dark
[Echo] (We glow in the dark)

[Verse 2]
I memorize the shadows tracing down your face
Lost in the warmth of your slow embrace
Every sigh that you make is a melody
Writing a song meant only for me

[Bridge]
Out in the rush of the world, it is so easy to hide
But here in the shadows, with you by my side
The walls fall away and it is only us two
Saeed found a heaven right here next to you
Holding you tight till the shadows all break
